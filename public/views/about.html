Poetry is great, but sometimes it's hard to write a whole idea to the end. However, losing some cool idea to the void sucks. I'm hoping this site can collect these lines, and through a little random luck, make some interesting poems out of them. In a similar vein, songs get stuck in heads. So let out those lyrics here too and maybe some interesting ideas will turn up.
